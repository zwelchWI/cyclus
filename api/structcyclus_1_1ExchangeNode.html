Detailed Description

ExchangeNodes are used in ExchangeGraphs to house information about a given translated Bid or Request.

Specifically, ExchangeNodes have a notion of unit capacities that the given Bid or Request contribute to the overall capacity of ExchangeNodeGroup. ExchangeNodes also have a notion of quantity, i.e., the maximum amount of a resource that can be attributed to it.
